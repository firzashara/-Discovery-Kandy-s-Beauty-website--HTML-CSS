Kandy Garisson Cemetery

The Kandy Garrison Cemetery, also known as the Kandy British Garrison Cemetery, is a historic cemetery located in Kandy, Sri Lanka. It is situated within the Kandy Garrison grounds, which were established during the British colonial period.

The cemetery is the final resting place for several British military personnel who served in Sri Lanka during the 19th and early 20th centuries. It is a significant historical site that reflects the colonial heritage of the country.

The graves in the Kandy Garrison Cemetery have elaborate tombstones and monuments, some of which bear inscriptions detailing the names, ranks, and regiments of the deceased. The cemetery provides insights into the military presence and the lives of the British officials and soldiers who served in Sri Lanka during that period.

Today, the Kandy Garrison Cemetery is maintained by the Commonwealth War Graves Commission, which oversees numerous military cemeteries around the world. The cemetery is open to the public and offers a glimpse into the colonial past of Sri Lanka. Visitors can explore the site, read the inscriptions on the gravestones, and reflect on the historical significance of the cemetery.
