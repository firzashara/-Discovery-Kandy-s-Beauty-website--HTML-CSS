Kandy View Point

Kandy, located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, is a beautiful city known for its scenic landscapes and cultural heritage. There are several viewpoints in and around Kandy that offer stunning panoramic views of the city and its surroundings. One popular viewpoint in Kandy is the Bahirawakanda Vihara Buddha Statue.

The Bahirawakanda Vihara Buddha Statue is a large white statue of the Buddha situated on a hilltop overlooking Kandy. It is one of the iconic landmarks of the city and provides an excellent vantage point to enjoy a panoramic view of Kandy and its lush green surroundings. To reach the viewpoint, visitors can either climb up the hill or take a short drive.

Another notable viewpoint in Kandy is the Arthur's Seat viewpoint. Located in the nearby town of Ampitiya, Arthur's Seat offers breathtaking views of Kandy and the surrounding mountain ranges. It is a popular spot for both locals and tourists to enjoy the sunset or capture stunning photographs.

In addition to these viewpoints, Kandy has several other scenic spots worth visiting. The Udawattakele Forest Reserve, located near the city center, offers nature trails and elevated viewpoints that provide a unique perspective of Kandy and its natural beauty. The Hanthana Mountain Range, situated a short distance away, is another popular destination for hiking and trekking, offering stunning views of Kandy and its surroundings from the mountaintop.
